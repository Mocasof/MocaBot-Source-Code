make a app

press on the bot tab

click on the create bot

now scorll down until you find bot perms

put the bot full perms

go to oauth2

change the auth method to in app auth

go to scopes

click on bot

dont go back to the bot tab on the oauth go to bot perms and do it admin

go to url generator

make the scopes bot

make the bot admin on the bot perms

copy the link and invite your bot to the server
